## Agent Quick Rules

**Q: I wrote a Humana PPO in FL — am I getting paid?**
- Check the contract-PBP number against the three batches above
- Also check the **effective date AND signature date** — Batch 2 and 3 have dual date requirements
- If in doubt, check with Humana RSM before submitting

**Q: H7617-107 — is it non-commissionable?**
- Yes, as of **April 1, 2026** for signatures on or after March 3, 2026
- Renewals still pay 2026 FMV

**Q: What about Humana PDPs?**
- S5884-105 is non-commissionable in FL for new sales in 2026
- Check the national Humana doc for other PDP plan IDs if needed
