# 2026 THEI Plan Grid — Overview
Source: 2026 THEI Plan Comparison Grid
Last updated: 2026-07-16

## Carrier Pros & Cons

### Aetna
**Pros:** Large Provider & Hospital Network
**Cons:** High costs for Tier 3 and Tier 4 meds

### CarePlus
**Pros:** Good local presence | Offers Food card | Rich dental
**Cons:** Limited availability outside S. Florida
**Notes:** Strong in Miami-Dade/Broward area

### Cigna (HealthSpring)
**Pros:** Baptist PCP option; | Freedom to use any dentist (no network restriction) | Implants
**Cons:** High copays for Tier 2- Tier 4 meds
**Notes:** Good for those who prioritize dental and Baptist network access

### Devoted
**Pros:** $150 annual reimbursement for health related equipment | Baptist specialists in network (PCP panel closed)
**Cons:** Relatively new carrier | Very high copays!
**Notes:** Rapidly expanding network in Florida

### Doctor’s
**Pros:** Includes a no referral plan | Robust Dental | Baptist Specialists in network | Low copays for meds | High OTC
**Cons:** Limited Network but growing | Relatively new carrier | Tier 4 drugs are high | Limited Dental providers
**Notes:** Niche plans; strong in specific counties

### Florida Blue
**Pros:** Well-known brand; statewide presence | Baptist PCP
**Cons:** Not competitive, high copays | No OTC

### Gold Kidney
**Pros:** Specialized care for kidney disease patients
**Cons:** Narrow appeal for general population | Limited network
**Notes:** Strong for CKD/ESRD patients

### HealthSun
**Pros:** Rich dental | High OTC &  food card | In-home support | Personal Hygiene allowance | PRP treatment | Therapeutic Massages
**Cons:** Limited Network for all providers including dentists
**Notes:** Good for members who like clinic settings (plan does have private doctors), don't mind limited network of drs, need dental and extra benefits

### Humana
**Pros:** Robust Dental benefits | Low Maximum Out of Pocket | In home support | Food card | Well known brand
**Cons:** “Network within Network” confusion
**Notes:** PCP receives specialist assignment list — may frustrate some members

### Simply
**Pros:** Robust Dental coverage | Very low copays | Food card | In-home support
**Cons:** Very Limited Network
**Notes:** Good for members who like clinic settings (plan does have private doctors), don't mind limited network of drs, need dental and extra benefits

### Solis
**Pros:** High OTC allowance | Food card | $1,000 flex card to use for additional dental, vision, hearing | In-home support
**Cons:** Limited Network
**Notes:** More limited than larger competitors

### UHC Preferred (PCP)
**Pros:** No referrals 2025 however, 2026 YES referrals | Large Provider & Hospital Network in BROWARD
**Cons:** No Baptist in Miami | Limited dental coverage
**Notes:** Simplified access to specialists

### UHC Medicare Max (PCN)
**Pros:** Bpatist PCP | Large Provider & Hospital Network In MIAMI
**Cons:** Limited dental coverage
**Notes:** Broad access; good option for frequent travelers

### WellCare
**Pros:** Low Max out of pocket | Allowance for gas, food, home imrpovement,
**Cons:** Limited Network
**Notes:** Good for budget-conscious members
