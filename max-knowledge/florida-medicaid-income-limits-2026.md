# Florida Medicaid & Medicare Savings Programs — 2026 Income Limits
**Source:** SSI-Related Programs & Coverage Groups, April 2026 (Florida SHINE)
**Updated:** July 2026

---

## Medicare Part B & A Key Figures (2026)
- Part B Premium: **$202.90/month** (standard)
- Part A: Free for most; $565/month if fewer than 30 quarters worked
- Part B Deductible: $283/year
- Part A Deductible: $1,736 per benefit period

---

## PROGRAMS MANAGED BY SOCIAL SECURITY (eff 01/01/2026)

### Supplemental Security Income (SSI)
- Federal Benefit Rate (FBR): **$994/month** (individual) | **$1,491/month** (couple)
- Asset Limit: $2,000 (individual) | $3,000 (couple)
- Includes Full Medicaid

### Low Income Subsidy (LIS) / Extra Help — 150% FPL
- Income: **$1,995/month** (individual) | **$2,705/month** (couple)
- Assets: $18,090 (individual) | $36,100 (couple) — includes burial exclusion
- Automatic with full Medicaid, QMB, SLMB, or QI1

---

## MEDICARE SAVINGS PROGRAMS / BUY-IN (interim 01/01/2026)
*Note: Figures do NOT include the standard $20 disregard*

### QMB — Qualified Medicare Beneficiary (100% FPL)
- **Pays:** Medicare Part A & B premiums, coinsurance & deductibles
- Income: **$1,330/month** (individual) | **$1,803/month** (couple)
- Assets: $9,950 (individual) | $14,910 (couple)

### SLMB — Specified Low-Income Medicare Beneficiary (120% FPL)
- **Pays:** Medicare Part B premium only
- Income: **$1,596/month** (individual) | **$2,164/month** (couple)
- Assets: Same as QMB

### QI1 — Qualifying Individual 1 (135% FPL)
- **Pays:** Medicare Part B premium only
- Income: **$1,796/month** (individual) | **$2,435/month** (couple)
- Assets: Same as QMB

### Working Disabled / QDWI (200% FPL)
- **Pays:** Medicare Part A premium only
- Must have lost SSDI due to employment
- Income: **$2,660/month** (individual) | **$3,607/month** (couple)
- Assets: $4,000 (individual) | $6,000 (couple)

## FLORIDA COMMUNITY MEDICAID (MEDS-AD) — 88% FPL (interim 04/01/2023)
- Income: **$1,182/month** (individual) | **$1,596/month** (couple)
- Assets: $5,000 (individual) | $6,000 (couple)

## INSTITUTIONAL / NURSING HOME PROGRAMS (eff 01/01/2026)

### ICP — Institutional Care Program
- Income: Up to **$2,982/month** (individual) | **$5,964/month** (couple)
- Assets: $2,000 (individual) | $3,000 (couple)

### Spousal Impoverishment (eff 07/01/2025)
- MMMNA: $2,644/month
- Maximum Income Allowance: $4,066.50/month
- Community Spouse Resource Allowance: **$162,660**
- Home Equity Limit: $752,000

### Transfer of Asset Divisor: $10,645 (eff 4/2025)

---

## STATE FUNDED PROGRAMS (eff 01/01/2026)

### Optional State Supplement (OSS) Redesign
- Maximum Payment: $184.40 (single) | $386.80 (couple)
- Income Limit: $1,072.40 (single) | $2,047.80 (couple)

### Home Care for Disabled Adults (HCDA)
- Income: Up to $2,982/month (individual) | $5,964/month (couple)

## STANDARD DISREGARDS
- Standard Disregard: $20/month
- Earned Income Disregard: $65 + ½ remaining earned income
- Student Earned Income: $2,416/month (max $9,460/year)

## DISABILITY
- SGA (non-blind): $1,690/month
- SGA (blind): $2,830/month

## AGENT USE NOTES
- QMB is the most common MSP — it eliminates Part B premium AND covers cost-sharing
- When a client qualifies for QMB, they ALSO get LIS/Extra Help automatically
- Always verify current eligibility — thresholds change annually in January
- Florida SHINE counselors assist with QMB/SLMB/QI1 applications
- Use this chart when a client asks "can the government pay my Medicare premium?"
